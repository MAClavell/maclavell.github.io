About Me

I love making games. Professionally, my career started at Vicarious Visions in 2020. Since then the company moved umbrellas from the Activision side to the Blizzard side and has become Blizzard Albany, where I work on the Diablo franchise.

I am an avid gamer who loves playing most generes of games (except horror!), but action games and RPGs always have a special place in my heart. That has translated to the tabletop genre as well, as I am an avid Dungeons and Dragons player and Dungeon Master. Getting to sit down and theorycraft a build in a new game or ideate a narrative moment for my tabletop game is one of my favorite things to do.

Take a seat and browse my projects. Don't be afraid to hit me up if you want to chat!
