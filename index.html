About Me

My name is Michael Clavell and I am an 18 year old programmer graduating in the spring of 2020 from Rochester Institute of Technology with a B.S. in Game Design and Development. I focus mostly on gameplay programming, creating a fun and engaging experience for the player. I also produce music and aim to integrate my knowledge of that subject into the development of games.

Since the seventh grade I've become enthralled by everything programming. Starting with Java and Lua, I quickly exanded to HTML5, CSS, and eventually C#. I have experience in beginner engines such as RPG Maker and GameMaker, as well as more powerful ones like Monogame and a little of Unreal Engine 4.

I started becoming a musician in my fourth grade with the drums and percussion. I continue playing them now and have also learned a bit of piano. Recently, digital music creation has become a little hobby of mine and I have gained experience in the programs Reason, Logic Pro, and FL Studio.
